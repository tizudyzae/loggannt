Help & onboarding

This companion page explains the workflow that used to sit in the hero cards. Bookmark it for screenshots, privacy notes, and PDF export tips.

Step 1 Drop a CSV

Use the Upload CSV button or drag a file anywhere on the viewer. The app caches the week locally so you can reopen it offline.

Step 2 Filter colleagues

Select individuals or tap the management preset. Every preview—schedule, line, and PDF—honors the same selection.

Step 3 Share polished sheets

When you are satisfied, export the PDF layouts. They mirror the existing reports, making handover painless.

Uploading a rota

Drop your CSV anywhere or use the floating Upload CSV button at the top of the main page.

The viewer validates headers instantly and stores the parsed data in localStorage only on your device.

  • Supported files follow the Mobile Rota export with comma-separated rows.
  • No data ever leaves the browser—ideal for GDPR-sensitive schedules.
  • Need to reset? Open the Saved weeks panel and clear cached entries.

Filtering the view

Use the Report scope list to toggle individuals. Keyboard shortcuts (Shift/Command) let you select multiple rows quickly.

The management preset highlights leads instantly when you tap it.

  • The schedule grid, condensed line chart, and PDF preview stay in sync.
  • Focus mode keeps exports tight—no unnecessary personal data.
  • Use the appearance controls to tweak highlight colors before exporting.

Exporting PDFs

Choose “PDF preview” inside the viewer to stage the layout, then press Export PDF.

The pdf-lib integration converts the SVG into landscape pages that managers already recognize.

  • Exports respect the current selection (entire week or focused colleague).
  • Print-ready pages land in your downloads folder within a second.
  • Use the system share sheet on mobile to hand over via Mail, WhatsApp, or Teams.
Why move the guidance here?
Keeping the main viewer minimal lets you focus on the data. This help hub now houses the narrative, screenshots, and compliance notes.
Is my data private?
Yes. The viewer never uploads anything; it stores schedules locally using localStorage. Clearing cached weeks removes every trace.
Need support?
Open an issue on the project repository or ping your engineering contact with the CSV you are trying to load.
